NotebookLM is a research tool powered by Large Language Models (LLMs) that acts as a virtual research assistant.

It automatically surfaces and summarizes information directly from your uploaded documents, helping you synthesize complex information and quickly find answers.

Key Action: Upload your research, notes, class materials, or other resources into NotebookLM to begin.

Benefits for Learning and Teaching

Saves Time & Effort

Automates summarizing and question answering from long documents.

Generates Ideas

Facilitates brainstorming and creative idea generation for projects or lessons.

Identifies Connections

Links concepts and insights across multiple uploaded sources.

Fosters Critical Thinking

Encourages deep analysis by finding contradictions or missing information.
